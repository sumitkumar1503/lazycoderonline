Our Story

From a YouTube Channel to a Coding Hub.

LazyCoder Online started in 2020 as a YouTube channel dedicated to helping Computer Science students understand complex programming concepts in a simple way. Founded by Sumit Kumar, a passionate software developer, the goal was simple: "Make coding lazy, efficient, and smart."

Over the years, we realized that students often struggle not with logic, but with setting up projects, configuring databases, and finding bug-free source code for their final year submissions.
